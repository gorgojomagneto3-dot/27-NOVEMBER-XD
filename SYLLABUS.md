## üìò B06 - BASIC SIX (World Link 1)

### UNIT 6: FREE TIME ACTIVITIES

**Skills:** Talk about hobbies, express preferences with gerunds, make/respond to invitations

## üìô B07 - BASIC SEVEN (World Link 2)
